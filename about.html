What the heck is SSISCSC?

Saigon South International School Computer Science Club (SSISCSC) is an unofficial club created for people in SSIS who are passionate about computer science. Computer science is the science of computation, a fancy way of saying taking some input, produce an output by running algorithms. If you love computer science, no matter it's hardware, software or theories. Furthermore you wish to be free (as in freedom), and *hacking* is your hobby. Come and join us! Summit a *pull-request* to chlin18@ssis.edu.vn if you want to be a member of SSISCSC :-)

TL;DR? Send an email to chlin18@ssis.edu.vn if you would like to join this club.
