What the hack is SSISCSC?

Saigon South International School Computer Science Club (SSISCSC) is an unofficial club created for people in SSIS who are passionate about computer science. Computer science is the science of computation, a fancy way to say taking some input, produce an output by running algorithms. If you love computer science, no matter if it's hardware, software or theories, and if you wish to be completely free (as in freedom), as well as *hacking* is your hobby, come and join us!

Submit a *pull-request* to chlin18@ssis.edu.vn if you want to change the digital world.
